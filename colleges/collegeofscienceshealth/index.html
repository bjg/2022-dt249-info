Why study Science at Technological University Dublin - City Campus

Welcome to the College of Sciences and Health. Technological University Dublin - City Campus has a well established reputation for providing students with an excellent learning experience on the road to careers in the sciences, health, computing, and mathematics. Our science programmes in Grangegorman give students an opportunity to acquire relevant professsional experience and develop the necessary practical skills needed for their careers.
