Welcome

Oifig na Gaeilge is responsible for ensuring the development of the use, visibility and status of the Irish language amongst the students and staff of Technological University Dublin.

find out more

Courses

We offer a range of classes and opportunities to obtain qualifications in the Irish language such as the Professional Certificate in Irish and the European Certificate in Irish (TEG).

Find out more

Translation

A translation service is available to staff across the University to ensure compliance with the Official Languages Act and to provide content for marketing and branding purposes.

Find out more

Student Initiatives

We organise initiatives to provide students the opportunity to use their Gaeilge socially and creatively e.g. Conas Atá TÚ (chat and cupán tae) and IrisZine (a bilingual e-zine produced by students for students).
