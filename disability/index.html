Disability Services

Disability Support Services

The goal of the Disability Support Services is to ensure that any student with a disability does not experience an educational disadvantage in accessing, fully participating in, and benefiting from, educational opportunities at TU Dublin city campus. To avail of our support during your studies, please register with our service.

 

IMPORTANT

The deadline for registering with the Disability Support Services, to ensure that your exam supports are in place for Semester I exams is:

Friday 5th November 2021

 

 

Watch our short videos to learn about the Disability Support Services and how to Register with our Service.
