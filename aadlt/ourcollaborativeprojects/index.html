    Our Collaborative Projects


    In forming the new Digital Campus & Learning Transformation Directorate, there is a need to bring together the skills and experience of colleagues within the three functions which make up the Directorate. Arising from one of a number of all staff ‘Town Hall’ meetings, the idea of creating a number of collaborative projects involving colleagues from all three functions was identified.

    4 collaborative projects were identified as follows

    1.    Develop a video introducing and explaining the Directorate

    2.    Development of the Directorate website

    3.    Survey of non-Webcourses eLearning activities across DIT

    4.    Social Media Utilisation – a pilot of using social media tools to develop student-friendly communications


    Each of these projects is now underway and it is anticipated that they will be completed in June 2017.
