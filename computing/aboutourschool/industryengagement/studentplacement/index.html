Student Placement

As part of many of our courses we offer students the opportunity to obtain industrial experience as part of their time with us.

While the details of these placement modules might differ slightly between programmes, the purpose remains the same, and the general guidelines as set out in the Industrial Placement Handbook apply to all placement programmes at the school.

 

Opportunities

The DTU857 B.Sc. in Computer Science programmes includes the option of a Seven-Month, Full-Time, Paid Industrial Placement for students. 

The TU259/TU262  Fundamentals of Computing Masters Qualifier PT/FT Conversion course includes a six-month industrial placement for students.

 

More details of what's specifically covered in the courses where internship is an optional module can be found at the links below:

http://www.tudublin.ie/catalogue/Programmes/Details/TU856

http://www.tudublin.ie/catalogue/Programmes/Details/TU857

 

External Examiners

As part of the continuous QA procedures at the DIT School of Computing, we need academic and industry representatives to sit on Programme, School and College review boards. These boards help to ensure the relevance and quality of all undergraduate and gradaute programmes taught at or taught by the School of Computing.

We also need external examiners to participate in the ongoing quality control of the assessment of modules taught and examined at the school.

For further details, please contact Iseult Kelly at soc-ieo@dit.ie.
