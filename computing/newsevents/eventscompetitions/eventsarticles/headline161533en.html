Computing Academy City Campus 2020

The School of Computer Science at TU Dublin is holding its annual Computing academy. Here, students from transition year will attend a week long course in TU Dublin, where they will learn a number of new and exciting Computer Science skills:

  • Programming Robots
  • Making Computer Games
  • Building Websites
  • Arduino programming

The Computing Academy takes place in TU Dublin City Campus from 25th - 28th May 2020.

All transition students are welcome to apply, please complete the application form by 5pm on Monday, 13th April 2020.

For more information, contact Iseult Kelly at soc-ieo@tudublin.ie.
