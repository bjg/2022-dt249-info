Welcome


Dr. Paul Doyle

Head of Computer Science

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to the School of Computer Science for the new academic year of 2021-2022. As the newly appointed Head of School, I look forward to meeting each of you over the coming weeks.

This academic year sees the return to campus life for many student groups, specifically the undergraduate full-time courses and our full-time MSc course. Our part-time and evening courses will operate a hybrid model, with teaching being primarily online for this semester. To facilitate a smooth transition to the new academic year we will run induction sessions for all students to introduce you to our newly opened, state-of-the-art facility in Grangegorman. These inductions are also provided to help answer all your queries regarding your programme.

For those of you who are joining us for the first time, and for those students returning to us, we look forward to meeting you shortly, and on behalf of the entire school I extend a warm welcome to you all.
