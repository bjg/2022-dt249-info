Ciaran Cawley

Background

Ciaran is a computer science lecturer in the School of Computer Science at TU Dublin (City Campu)s with over a decade of experience in Software Engineering within private industry, most recently managing a global software development project in the area of online education platforms. Ciaran previously lectured in the Dundalk Institue of Technology in the areas of Software Development and Enterprise Systems. Ciaran received his BSc from University Collge Dublin and subsequently an MEng in Computer Systems from the University of Limerick.

Research Interests

Development & Management of Software Intensive Systems 

Software Visualistion 

Blended Learning
