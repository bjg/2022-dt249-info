Dr Pierpaolo Dondio

Background

I joined the School of Computer Science full time in 2012. My main research areas are Multi-Agent Systems, Reasoning Under Uncertainty and Argumentation Theory. I also investigate Online Communities behaviour and Dynamics.

2012-            Lecturer, School of Computer Science, TU Dublin

2010-2012     Senior Research Analyst, AON Center for Analytics and Innovation

2005-2010     Post-doctoral Research Fellow and PHD Student at Trinity College Dublin

2002-2004     IT Specialist, IBM
