Class Reunions

Thinking about organising a Class Reunion at TU Dublin?

If you would like to organise a reunion, the Alumni Office can reach out to your classmates on your behalf and ask them to contact you directly.  E-mail us your request.

If you would like to have a reception at your old School, we can book a reception room for you Monday – Thursday.

If you would like a tour of the new Grangegorman campus as part of your reunion, please let us know.

If you have organised your own reunion, please send us photographs for our website and Facebook page.
